Color Cheat Sheet

Hue: A color or shade that is discernible by red, green, etc.

Value, Shade: The amount of black in the color.


Tint, Tone: The amount of white in the color.

  • Primary Colors: Red, Yellow and Blue

  • Secondary Colors: Green, Orange and Purple

Complementary Colors: Colors that are across from each other on the color wheel.

Analagous Colors: Colors that are next to each other on the color wheel.
