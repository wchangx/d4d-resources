Design for Developers

Visual Design Principles

  • Alignment

    The placement of elements such that edges line up along common rows or columns, or their bodies along a common center.

  • Emphasis

    Creates interest and pulls the user’s attention toward the focal point.

  • Hierarchy

    The relationship between objects in a composition.

  • Consistency

    The usability of a system is improved when similar parts are expressed in similar ways.

  • White space

    The unmarked space between elements in a composition.

  • Balance

    A composition is balanced when all elements have optical equilibrium.

  • Unity

    When all elements are in agreement, and no individual part is viewed as more important than the whole design.
