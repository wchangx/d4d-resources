Typography Cheat Sheet

Typeface

A typeface is a set of characters that share stylistic unity. It is comprised of an alphabet of letters, numbers, punctuation and diacritical marks.

Font

A complete character set of a particular typeface in one size.
Also, an electronic file that renders a typeface in all sizes.

  • Ascender

    The portion of the character that extends above the median.

  • Descender

    The portion of the character that extends below the median.

  • Counter

    An area entirely or partially closed by a letter form.

  • Baseline

    Units are building blocks of the grid. They are uniform in size.

  • Median

    Equal spacing between units.

  • x-height

    A combination of a number of units and gutters.

  • Line-height

    The distance between two baselines.

Line-height

Stay between 1.3em and 1.6em for legibility.
